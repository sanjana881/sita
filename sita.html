DIETARY RECCOMENDATION

NUTRITION FOR THE MENSTRUAL PHASE



What is happening?
At this stage,estrogen and progesterone levels are low. The lining of the uterus, called the endometrium, is shed, causing bleeding.

FOOD FOCUS:Add nutrients; warmth and comfort


MUST INCLUDE:
  • Sea vegetables e.g. kelp
  • Sweet potato
  • Activated brown rice
  • Kefir or probiotic yoghurts
  • Pumpkin seeds
  • Millet- based cereals
  • Wheat germ
  • Protein of choice; beef, chicken, lentils, fish, eggs, tofu
  • Nuts

NUTRITION FOR THE FOLLICULAR PHASE



what is happening?
At this stage,Estrogen and progesterone are on the rise.

FOOD FOCUS:Fresh and light

MUST INCLUDE:
  • Salad vegetables
  • Flaxseeds
  • Avocado
  • Broccoli
  • Nuts/seed mix
  • Probiotic yoghurt
  • Zucchini/ courgettes
  • Buckwheat
  • Salmon
  • Kefir

NUTRITION FOR THE OVULATORY PHASE



what is happening?
At this stage,estrogen peaks.Testosterone and progesterone levels are on the rise.

FOOD FOCUS:Fibrous and light

MUST INCLUDE:
  • Quinoa
  • Eggs
  • Kale
  • Radishes
  • Wholegrain: breads, pasta, rice –- BB vitamins
  • Fruits: berries, citrus, papaya

NUTRITION FOR THE LUTEAL PHASE



what is happening?
Estrogen and progesterone levels are high. If the egg isn’t fertilized, hormone levels decrease and the menstrual cycle starts again.

FOOD FOCUS:Curb cravings

MUST INCLUDE:
  • Cauliflower
  • Cucumber (water retention)
  • Squash
  • Caffeine- free herbal teas
  • Sesame seeds
  • Spinach
  • Brown rice
  • Protein of choice: tofu, chicken, lean meats, fish and seafood
  • Berries
  • Turmeric latte blend
  • Dark chocolate
